About Me

I am a Geospatial Information Science student at Maseno University, passionate about harnessing spatial data, remote sensing, and GeoAI to address environmental and urban challenges. My work focuses on applying GIS, data analytics, and spatial modeling to real-world problems such as air quality mapping, climate vulnerability assessment, and urban resilience planning.

With hands-on experience using tools like QGIS, Google Earth Engine, Python, and Web GIS, I build geospatial solutions that are practical, data-driven, and impactful. I am committed to transforming complex datasets into clear maps, insights, and decision‑ready outputs that support sustainable development goals, environmental management, and evidence‑based planning.
